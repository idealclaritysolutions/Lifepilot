Delete Your Account

Life Pilot AI by Ideal Clarity Solutions LLC

You can delete your Life Pilot AI account and all associated data directly from the app. Follow the steps below to permanently remove your account.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Life Pilot AI on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon in the top-right corner).
  3. Scroll down to the account section.
  4. Tap "Delete my account and data".
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted. You will be asked to confirm twice to prevent accidental deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  1. Account information — your email address, name, and authentication credentials.
  2. Tasks and goals — all items on your board, including pending, completed, and archived items.
  3. Journal entries — all journal entries and reflections.
  4. Habits — all habit definitions and completion history.
  5. People and events — all contacts, birthdays, and events you have saved.
  6. Chat history — all messages with the AI assistant.
  7. Shared lists — any shared lists you created (other members will lose access).
  8. Push notification subscriptions — your notification preferences and tokens.
  9. Subscription information — your plan and promo code records.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Local data stored on your device will remain until you clear the app's cache or uninstall it.

Data Retained After Deletion

Local device data: Any data stored locally on your device (offline cache) will remain until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app. This data is not accessible to us and exists only on your device.

Shared list contributions: Items you added to shared lists owned by other users may remain visible to those list members, but your name and account association will be removed.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account or need assistance with deletion, contact us at support@getlifepilot.app and we will process your request within 7 business days.
